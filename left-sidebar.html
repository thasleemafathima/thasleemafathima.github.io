Solar-Quote

Created as a part of naveco-power website for savings calculations

This application lets the users provide their location and monthly energy bill. Then the application does all the required calculations to display the energy production graphs and saving summery tables with estimation for 25 years. Those calculations consider the location electricity rates, system degradation and power escalation rate. Moreover, the application gives the users a financing estimation based on their needs.

User Location

With the address input provided by the user, we take in the latitude, longitude, climatic condition. To calculate the solar energy in that particular area.

Monthly Bill and Offset

On user entering their monthly electric bill and offsets, It calculate the saving summary on using solar energy. It also provide them with available financing option. It use graphic representation to represent the production over 25 years of estimation. Have used React, NodeJs, material UI, Axios, GitHub and Azure Devops to create this application.
